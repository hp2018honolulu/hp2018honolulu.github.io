Venue information

The 2018 workshop will take place in Honolulu, Hawaii, on the island of Oahu. The main event will be hosted at the Ala Moana Hotel:

  • Ala Moana Hotel
  • 410 Atkinson Drive, Honolulu, Hawaii 96814
  • Phone: 808-955-4811 | Fax: 808-944-6839
  • Email: reservations@alamoanahotel.com
  • http://www.alamoanahotelhonolulu.com/
The hotel is conveniently located about 8 miles (20 minutes by taxi) from the Honolulu International Airport (HNL), next to the Ala Moana Center, the largest shopping mall in Hawaii, featuring over 350 shops and restaurants, and walking distance from Ala Moana Beach Park.
