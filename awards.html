Young Scientist Travel Awards

Purpose

The Organizing Committee of the 2018 IUCr HP workshop in Honolulu has some funding available for Young Scientist Travel Awards. These awards will provide funding for young and early-career high-pressure researchers to offset the cost of participation in the workshop.

Award

Limited number of Young Scientist Travel Awards will be awarded, based on scientific merit and financial need. Funds may be applied only for registration, travel, and accommodations and are restricted to traveling to the 2018 Honolulu workshop. Each travel award will equal $1000. The travel awards will be processed as reimbursement, after submission of original expense receipts to the UH Office of Conference Services.

Eligibility

The awards are intended for young (under 35) scientists who have graduate student or postdoctoral appointments. Preference will be given to applicants making a presentation (oral or poster) at the workshop. The 2018 Organizing Committee will oversee the application review and select awardees.

Application Deadline

For consideration, all application materials must be received by the application close date of April 15, 2018. Late applications will not be accepted.

Application Materials

To apply for the 2018 Honolulu workshop Young Scientist Travel Award, please submit the following by email to gjfinkel@hawaii.edu:

  • - A short biography for publication on the workshop website (100 words or less + photo)
  • - Current resume including your permanent address (in pdf format)
  • - Letter of recommendation (1 page) from thesis advisor or postdoctoral supervisor

    For more information about Young Scientist Travel Awards, please contact gjfinkel@hawaii.edu
